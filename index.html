mvp

raw

You tell us what you want from your product. Tell us everything you envisage it being able to achieve and the business reasons behind it. We don't care how crazy it is - in fact we encourage it...

refine

We break down those initial ideas and work with you to identify and prioritise what the application does. MVP (Minimum Viable Product) will guide us, and have us up and running fast.

extract

Identifying the MVP we get straight to work... Our aim to give you something to get out to the user as fast as possible so that it can be used. This then helps drive future development and direction.

ship

The end product is bottled and put on ice. We can start thinking about further development with clear vision and mind. This keeps our projects productive and fast moving, and happily for you low cost with no obligations to continue.

get it out there...

Our whole methodology relies upon getting something in front of the user as soon as possible. Even if it is something quite simple. You may be surprised where this takes us as you can never anticipate fully what the user wants or tries to do with what you give them. At least now you can rapidly adapt and respond to how the product is most valuable.
